HOW TO PLAY

The goal

Reduce the enemy hero to 0 HP — by force of arms, or by summoning your Abstract: the living concept your deck serves. Fill your Invoke meter to 10 and the Abstract takes form at the centre of your geometry, with devastating powers.

Turns

  • Each turn you gain a mana crystal (up to 10), refill mana, and draw a card.
  • Drag followers from your hand onto the empty nodes (dots) of your geometry. Each node grants a different bonus — hover a dot to see it.
  • Spells are cast by clicking them; they cost mana and resolve immediately (some need a target).

Acting with followers

Each follower may act once per turn — but not the turn it is played (it shows a zZ badge while it rests). Click a glowing follower and all valid attack targets light up:

  • Attack — click an enemy follower, their Abstract, or their hero. Followers in combat deal damage to each other.
  • Invoke — instead of attacking, press the violet Invoke button to channel the follower's invoke value (its violet badge) into your Abstract's meter.

Press Esc, click Cancel, or click empty ground to deselect. Every turn is the same dilemma: press the attack, hold the line, or hasten the summoning.

The Abstract

Once your meter reaches 10, the Summon Abstract button appears. The Abstract enters at the centre, triggers its arrival power, exerts a passive aura at the start of each of your turns, and can attack like a follower. It can be killed — and it will not return.

The geometries

Fear — a pentagon: five versatile nodes for a creeping, attritional game.
Justice — a triangle: only three nodes, but each blessing is mighty.
Knowledge — a hexagon: six nodes of small, compounding advantages.
